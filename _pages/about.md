Hi, my name is **Chen-Chieh Liao (廖振傑)**, an Assistant Professor at the
  <a href="https://www.isct.ac.jp/en/">Institute of Science Tokyo</a>. I also serve as a Guest Researcher at
  <a href="https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/">The University of Tokyo</a> and as a Data Scientist at Rinji Advice Co., Ltd.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Institute of Science Tokyo under the supervision of Prof. [Hideki Koike](https://www.vogue.cs.titech.ac.jp/koike). Before my current position, I worked as a Researcher at the Institute of Science Tokyo, an Associate Researcher at [Cygames](https://www.cygames.co.jp/en/), a Visiting Researcher at [Keio University](https://www.kmd.keio.ac.jp/), an AI Researcher at [NAVER Cloud](https://www.navercloudcorp.com/lang/en/), and an Algorithm Engineer at [ACES, Inc.](https://acesinc.co.jp/). I also received my B.E. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 2020 and 2022.

My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Machine Learning, and 3D Vision. I build intelligent interactive systems for skill acquisition, with a particular focus on pose prediction for sports training, 3D body and hand pose estimation, and VR/AR visualization techniques for learning and analysis.
